Copy tradeoffs: Variant 1 copy is direct and non-blaming. "Use the project selector" is more actionable than "select a project." Variant 2/owner is factual without urgency. Variant 2/member avoids implying a bug — saying "you're not in any project" is accurate and avoids confusion.

Affordance tradeoffs: The single-CTA card is frictionless and familiar — users know what to do. Downside: the card floats freely in a potentially large content area, which may read as "something is loading." Consider adding a subtle background pattern or reducing padding if the content area is very tall. The card pattern works well at all three breakpoints without reflow changes.
