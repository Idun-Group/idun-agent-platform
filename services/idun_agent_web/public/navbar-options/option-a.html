Tradeoffs

ProsBoth selectors are fully labelled at rest. The purple label creates immediate brand tie-in. The stacked layout adds no horizontal space — it only grows height by ~12 px. Works at 375 px by abbreviating the label ("WS", "Proj").
ConsLabel is tiny (~9 px). Two-line height may feel taller than single-line options at certain zoom levels. Users with poor eyesight may not read the label without magnification.
Best forDense professional UIs where horizontal header space is constrained and brand color use is intentional. Pairs well with the current Idun purple-primary design.
