Verdict

Pros

  • Immediately scannable — variant is obvious before reading text
  • Colored icon circle reinforces severity without being aggressive
  • Progress bar tells users how long the toast will live
  • Clean in both light and dark modes — tint adapts well
  • Three layers is still visually manageable (fill + border + bar)

Cons

  • Color tint may clash with certain page-level accent colors
  • In light mode, subtle tints can look washed out on white backgrounds
  • More visually heavy than Option A — may feel busy in dense UIs
  • Progress bar adds a third element to style and keep in sync

Best for

  • Consumer-facing platforms where clarity and urgency matter
  • Users who need to react to errors quickly without reading text
  • Design systems that use color heavily for feedback states
  • Apps where the auto-dismiss timing UX (progress bar) is valued
